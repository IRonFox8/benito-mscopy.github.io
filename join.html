How To Join Our School Clubs

FBLA Future Business Leaders of America

Become A Member!

Become a part of Benito's first-ever FBLA chapter and join the excitement. It's your chance to develop your leadership skills, make lasting memories, become the leaders of tomorrow, and be recognized for your hard work. We can't wait to see what you'll accomplish next!

How To Join:
  1. Sign Up: Fill out a simple membership form available in the school office or online.
  2. Attend Meetings: Be sure to attend our regular meetings where we discuss upcoming events and projects.
  3. Get Involved: Participate in our monthly meeting, events and competitions.
  4. Grow as a Leader: Take on new responsinilities and make a real impact in our club!


Ready to start your journey?

Contact Mrs. Perez or stop by the school office to learn more about joining FBLA at Benito Middle School.

NJHS National Junior Honor Society

Become A Member!

NJHS is open to students who excel in Scholarship, Leadership, Service, Character, and Citizenship. Membership is by invitation. Students are invited to join based on their GPA and extracurricular involvement. If you meet the criteria, don't wait, apply today!

How To Join:
  1. Meet the Criteria: Maintain a high GPA and demonstrate leadership, character, and service.
  2. Submit Your Application: Keep an eye out for application details shared by the NJHS advisor or school office.
  3. Induction Ceremony: Once selected, you'll be invited to the Induction Ceremony where you will officially become a member of NJHS.
  4. Get Involved: Jump right in and begin contributing to impactful service projects and leadership activities!

Ready to start your journey?

Reach out to Mrs. Olson or visit the school office for more information.

PTSA Parent Teacher Student Association

Become A Member!

At our school, we believe that when parents, teachers, and students come together, amazing things happen! The PTSA is where we unite to create a vibrant, supportive, and thriving school community. When you join the PTSA, you're not just a member, you're a key player in shaping our school's future!

How To Join:

  2. Get Involved : Whether you can donate time, ideas, or skills, every contribution counts.
