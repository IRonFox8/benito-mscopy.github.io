Future Business Leader of America

Empowering Tomorrow's Leader!



Driving the future of Business Leadership and Innovation!

Step into the world of business and leadership with FBLA (Future Business Leaders of America) at Benito Middle School! This dynamic club empowers students to develop essential skills for success in the business world. From networking and teamwork to leadership and public speaking, FBLA offers endless opportunities for growth.


Exciting Activities!

As an FBLA member, you'll take part in monthly meetings, where we engage in fun and educational activities, discuss upcoming events, and prepare for competitions. Additionnally, our induction ceremony was a special event where new members were officially welcomed into the FBLA family, creating a strong foundation for a year filled with leadership opportunities and growth.


Incredible Achievements Already!

This is an exciting year for Benito Middle School as we participate in FBLA for the first time! Our FBLA members have already made their mark! Out of the 122 students who competed at the District level, a remarkable 55 students won awards for their excellence in business and leadership skills. We are so proud of their hard work and dedication!
The next challenge awaits: The State Conference in Orlando at the end of March, where our talented members will compete for top honors on a state-wide level.
Stay tuned for more incredible achievements from our future business leaders!


Empower yourself and join FBLA today!

National Junior Honor Society

Lead with Purpose, serve with Heart!



Excellence, Leadership, and Service!

The National Junior Honor Society (NJHS) at Benito Middle School is dedicated to recognizing students who demonstrate excellence in scholarship, leadership, service, character, and citizenship. Becoming a member of NJHS is a prestigious honor and a chance to contribute positively to our school and community.


Activities that Make a Difference! Being a member of NJHS means being actively involved in projects that positively impact our community. Here are just a few of the amazing events our members participate in:
  • Toys Drive for Advent Health: Spread holiday cheer by collecting toys for children in need.
  • Book Drive for Africa: Providing educational resources to children in underserved communities.
  • Hygiene Drive for Metropolitan Ministries: Collecting hygiene products to help those facing hardship.
  • Pet Supply Drive: Supporting local animal shelters by donating essential pet supplies.
  • Drive for Foster Kids: Ensuring children in foster care have the clothing they need to thrive.

Achievements to Be Proud Of

  • Dedication to Service: Our NJHS members have devoted hundreds of service hours to supporting our local community, leaving a lasting impact on those we help.
  • Leadership by Example: Through service projects and leadership roles, our members exemplify the values of responsibility, integrity, and teamwork, making a difference in the lives of others.
  • A Tradition of Giving Back: We've built a strong tradition of community involvement, creating positive changes that resonate throughout Benito Middle School and beyond.

Join NJHS and become part of something special!

Benito Middle School Sports Club

Where Your Sports Dreams Come Alive


Push your limits and reach rew heights!
At Benito Middle School Sports Club, we believe in nurturing and fostering a love for sports. Whether you're a seasoned athlete or just starting out, our clubs offers a wide range of activities to suit every interest and skill level. From basketball and soccer to track and field, there's something for everyone!


Exciting Activities! The Sports Club at Benito Middle School offers an exciting range of activities for all athletes:
  • Basketball: The club runs practice drills focused on shooting, dribbling, defensive techniques, and Team-building activities.
  • Volleyball: The club holds regular practices where players can work on passing, serving, and spiking techniques. Coaches help students improve their volleyball skills, and friendly matches are organized to give players the chance to compete in a fun, supportive environment.
  • Track and Field: In track and field, students engage in various training exercises tailored to sprints, long-distance running, jumping, and throwing events.
  • Soccer: Practices focus on developing core soccer skills, such as dribbling, passing, shooting, and defensive techniques. Players engage in drills to improve their speed, coordination, and teamwork.
  • Football: Football practices focus on building strength, agility, and teamwork. The club organizes training sessions where players can learn plays, improve their footwork, and practice their tackling skills.

Celebrate Achievements!
  • Athletes who shine: Our athletes excel both on and off the field, consistently achieving greatness with numerous district championships and personal bests.
  • Victory against Liberty Middle: Benito Middle School triumphed in both track and soccer, securing impressive victories against Liberty Middle School.
  • A legacy of excellence: With every victory celebrated and every effort recognized, our Sports Club members are building a legacy of success and teamwork.

Join Benito's Outstanding Sports Club today and be a Champion!

Book Club

Where Reading Comes to Life!



Discover new worlds and explore endless adventures in our Book Club!

The Book Club at Benito Middle School is the perfect place for students who love to read and share stories! Whether you're into fiction, mystery, or adventure, our club offers a welcoming space to explore a variety of genres.


Exciting activities in our Book Club! At Benito Middle School's Book Club, we believe reading is an adventure, and we make it exciting! As a member, you'll participate in a variety of fun and engaging activities, including:
  • Poetry & Pizza Night: Join us every month for an evening of poetry readings and pizza! It's the perfect way to explore creative writing, share your favorite poems, and enjoy a delicious meal with friends.
  • Buddy Reading: Pick your next great read from our Buddy Reading Display, then partner with a buddy to read and discuss your book together. It's a great way to discover new stories while bonding with fellow readers.
  • Reading Contest: Take on the challenge each month! Choose 10 or more books, write a review for each one, and you'll be invited to a fun Ice Cream Party as a reward for your reading achievements. It's a delicious way to celebrate your love of books!

Our Achievements

  • Expanding our literary horizons: Our club has enjoyed exploring a wide range of genres from mystery to fantasy, poetry, and beyond!
  • Building a reading community: Through shared discussions and events, we've fostered a space where students can connect with others who love books just as much as they do.
  • Supporting reading in our community: Our club actively promotes reading culture by engaging with school events and organizing book-related drives and activities.

Join our Book Club today and discover your next favorite story!!

Orchestra Club

Discover Your Passion for Music and Make Beautiful Melodies With Us!



Let the Music Shine!

The Orchestra Club at Benito Middle School is where music comes alive! Students come together to create beautiful melodies, learning to play instruments, improve their skills, and perform in a collaborative, supportive environment. Whether you're a seasoned musician or just starting out, the club offers a chance to grow, have fun, and be part of something special.


Activities that Make a Difference

The Orchestra Club is your chance to create beautiful music, build lasting friendships, and perform in exciting events!
In the Orchestra Club, you'll have the opportunity to master your instrument, collaborate with fellow musicians, and prepare for exciting performances at school concerts, community events, and special showcases! Each rehearsal is a chance to improve your skills, explore new musical pieces, and create harmonious music together.


Incredible Achievements

  • Showcasing musical talent: Our orchestra has performed at multiple school events, captivating audiences with breathtaking performances that highlight our students' hard work and skill.
  • Expanding horizons: Under the guidance of Mrs. Prather, our orchestra has proudly participated in prestigious events like All County, FMEA (Florida Music Education Association), and All State, earning recognition for their outstanding performances.
  • Fostering growth and connection: Throught rehearsals and performances, students have honed their musical talents while building lasting friendships within the club.
  • Enriching the school community: Our orchestra club continues to bring the joy of music to our school, creating memorable experiences and enriching the cultural atmosphere of our events.

Join our Orchestra Club today and make beautiful music with us!

Robotics Club


Transform Ideas into Reality in Our Robotics Club!



Ready to create the future?

The Robotics Club at Benito Middle School is a place where creativity meets technology! Here, students collaborate to design, build, and program robots, gaining hands-on experience in engineering, coding, and problem-solving. Whether you're new to robotics or an experienced tech enthusiast, the club offers an exciting opportunity to learn, innovate, and compete in fun challenges.


Activities that Make a Difference

As a member of the Robotics Club, you'll dive into hands-on projects that allow you to design, build, and program robots from the ground up!

Every Tuesday during 7th grade lunchtime, we come together to tackle exciting challenges, collaborate on innovative solutions, and refine our skills in coding, engineering, and problem-solving.


Achievements to Be Proud Of

  • Innovating with technology: Our Robotics Club members are already making waves, designing and building our very first robots as we gear up for upcoming challenges.
  • Passion and dedication: The commitment and enthusiasm of our team members shine through in every project, pushing the boundaries of what we can achieve together.
  • Ready for competition: With the first competition just around the corner, we're excited to showcase our creativity, problem-solving skills, and teamwork to the world!


Join our Robotics Club today and build the future with us!
